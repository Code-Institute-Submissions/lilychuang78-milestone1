About Me

My name is Lily Chuang. My passion is to be the interface between human and machine. I have worked as a developer for many years and developed multiple models of voice control systems for automobile brands, such as Volkswagen, Porsche and Audi. Learning makes me happy! I decided to study and become a software developer in my spare time. I can say myself a dedicated and motivating person. Besides that, I speaks four languages, Mandarin, Japanese, English and some German. I also consider myself to be international as I have lived in more than six countries in my life time. As a result, I am a very compassionate and sensitive toward individual differences. I believe communication is not only important among people but even so in the future of machine-human communication!
